NXspindispersion¶

Status:

base class, extends NXobject

Description:

Draft subclass of NXelectronanalyser to describe the spin filters in photoemission experiments.

Symbols:

No symbol table

Groups cited:

NXdeflector, NXlens

Structure:

spin_filter_type: (optional) NX_CHAR

Type of spin detector, VLEED, SPLEED, Mott, etc.

spin_filter_FoM: (optional) NX_FLOAT {units=NX_DIMENSIONLESS}

Figure of merit of the spin detector

spin_filter_shermann_function: (optional) NX_FLOAT {units=NX_DIMENSIONLESS}

Effective Shermann function, calibrated spin selectivity factor

spin_scattering_energy: (optional) NX_FLOAT {units=NX_ENERGY}

Energy of the spin-selective scattering

spin_scattering_angle: (optional) NX_FLOAT {units=NX_ANGLE}

Angle of the spin-selective scattering

spin_target: (optional) NX_CHAR

Name of the target

spin_target_preparation: (optional) NX_CHAR

Preparation procedure of the spin target

spin_target_preparation_date: (optional) NX_DATE_TIME

Date of last preparation of the spin target

DEFLECTOR: (optional) NXdeflector

deflectors in the spin dispersive section

LENS: (optional) NXlens

Individual lenses in the spin dispersive section
